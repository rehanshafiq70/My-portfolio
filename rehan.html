Who Am I?

A passionate problem-solver at the crossroads of AI and life science.

I'm Rehan Shafique, an AI Developer and Bioinformatics student at the University of Agriculture Faisalabad (UAF), Pakistan. I specialise in building end-to-end machine learning pipelines and deploying intelligent web applications that solve real-world healthcare and research challenges.

My academic foundation in Bioinformatics gives me a unique lens through which I apply Deep Learning to genomics, proteomics, and medical imaging. My flagship project — a Skin Cancer Prediction System — exemplifies my commitment to AI for social good.

Beyond coding, I contribute to open-source, write technical articles, and mentor junior developers. I believe the future of medicine will be written in Python.
